Know Your Elementals

Water

When balanced, water can be sensible and lucid. When in excess, water can become demanding and covetous. When deficient, water can completely withdraw from the world. This element is responsible for nurturing life, but when aggrivated it is responsible for taking it away.

Fire

The balanced fire element excels at communication and is lively, creative, and sanguine. It brings passion and flair to whatever it touches. In excess, fire can become erratic and overly excitable. When deficient, it can become highly dangerous. This element is responsible for explosions, and death when aggrivated.

Air

Earth

Earth is grounded and practical. When balanced, Earth is the glue that binds all elements together. It is nurturing, supportive, relaxed, oriented, and attentive. Unbalanced "Excess" Earth, however, frets and meddles and can be quite overbearing as that wonderful groundedness transforms into unmovable and impractical stubbornness. When "Deficient", Earth can become vacillating.
